Porridge

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Quantity
Oats 1/2 cup
Milk or Water 1 cup
Salt A pinch
Butter or Honey To taste

Steps:

1. Pour milk or water into a small pot and bring to a boil.

2. Add oats and a pinch of salt.

3. Cook on low heat for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally.

4. Add butter or honey before serving.
